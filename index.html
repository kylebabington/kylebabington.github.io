About

I’m a hands-on, visual learner who loves building things that make life simpler and more accessible. Curiosity drives me — whether I’m exploring the wilderness or experimenting with new tech, I’m always looking for smarter, kinder ways to connect people with the world around them.

I believe technology should serve everyone. My work mixes practical tinkering with empathy — shipping full-stack tools that help people navigate family life, home care, and health with more clarity.
